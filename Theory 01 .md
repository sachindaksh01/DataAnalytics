### **What is Data Analytics?**

**Data Analytics** means **looking at information carefully to understand something and make better decisions.**

It is like **learning from past data**.

---

### **ğŸ› Shop Example (Very Simple)**

Imagine you have a **small shop**.

Every day you write:

* How many customers came
* Which items were sold the most
* How much money you earned

After **1 month**, you check your notebook.

You notice:

* Most customers come in the **evening**
* **Milk and Bread** sell the most
* Sales are **low on Monday**
* Sales are **high on Saturday and Sunday**

Now you make decisions:

* Keep **more Milk and Bread** in stock
* Add **more staff** in the evening time
* Give **small offers** on weekends

This is **Data Analytics**.
